# Impact of Credit Scoring Models and Big Data Analytics on Loan Approvals

## Overview
This project examines how credit scoring models and big data analytics influence loan approval decisions in the Indian banking sector.

## Objectives
- Understand credit scoring models
- Analyze the role of big data analytics in banking
- Study the impact on loan approval decisions
- Evaluate benefits and challenges of analytics-driven lending

## Technologies & Concepts
- Credit Scoring Models
- Big Data Analytics
- Credit Risk Management
- Digital Lending
- Banking Analytics

## Methodology
The study is based on secondary data collected from:
- RBI Reports
- Banking Publications
- Research Journals
- Financial Reports

## Key Findings
- Improved loan approval accuracy
- Reduced credit risk
- Faster loan processing
- Better customer experience
- Enhanced financial inclusion
